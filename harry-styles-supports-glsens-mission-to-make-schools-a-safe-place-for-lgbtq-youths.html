Harry Styles Supports GLSEN's mission to make schools a safe place for LGBTQ Youths

Harry Styles team at HSHQ broke the good news of two Pride exclusive shirts being sold via their Twitter.

The proceeds of the shirt will go to support GLSEN. GLSEN is a charitable organization that strives to make all schools a safe and inclusive learning environment for LGBTQ youths.

In celebration of Pride, two special Treat People With Kindness T-Shirts have been added to the online store for the month of June. All proceeds will go to support @GLSEN's work ensuring safe & inclusive schools for LGBTQ youth. https://t.co/CtzBuWwh4T pic.twitter.com/0YiJz1LmZo

— HSHQ (@HSHQ) June 5, 2018

Harry then retweeted it to get the word spread further. The two Pride exclusive shirts say “Treat People With Kindness” on them in rainbow colouring. The shirts have sizes ranging from S to 2XL, come in black or white, and are $34.95. Shirts can be purchased here. Harry has a long history of donating a portion of sales via his Treat People With Kindness merchandise which donates to various charities including Children’s Cancer Society, Action Against Hunger, Birmingham Children’s Hospital, and many more local charities.

According to Harry’s website, fans can:

“[purchase an] exclusive Treat People With Kindness hair tie set. All of Harry’s profits from these purchases, as well as a portion of ticket sales, will be donated to various local charities from his tour stops around the world.”

Styles is also well known among his fans for supporting the LGBTQ community before this by holding up various Pride flags during concerts, praising states who voted for LGBTQ marriage and posting his support on social media.

Tickets for the remainder of Harry’s tour can be found here.
