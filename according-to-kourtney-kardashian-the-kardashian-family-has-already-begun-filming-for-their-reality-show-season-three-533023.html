According To Kourtney Kardashian, The Kardashian Family Has Already Begun Filming For Their Reality

Kourtney Kardashian announced filming on season 3 of her reality show was underway just weeks before the second season of the Hulu series premiered.

I’ve been filming season three here, Kardashian said to E! News during her visit to New York to launch her Boohoo by Kourtney Kardashian Barker collection at New York Fashion Week on Tuesday.

They are currently filming season three, she continued. In anticipation of the upcoming second season, Kardashian, 43, gave fans a sneak peek at her relationship with her new husband, Travis Barker.

In Milan, Travis and I will have our wedding fittings. As she continued, “the season displays everything leading up until,” I realized that she was referring to the upcoming season.

Season 2 of Keeping Up with the Kar-Jenners is set to premiere in just a few days, giving viewers another glimpse at the family’s highs and lows, including matriarch Kris Jenner’s (age 66) recent health scare.

Teaser footage from July shows Kris crying while talking about a mysterious health risk. As seen above in a hospital bed, Kris says, “I can’t tell my kids I’m terrified.” They already have a lot on their plate. They shouldn’t be concerned about Mom.

Khloé Kardashian, the third daughter, seems to sense something is wrong, stating, “I don’t know what’s going with you.” Keeping Up with the Kardashians is an E! reality show that has followed the family since its inception in 2007.

In September 2020, it was reported that KUWTK would be ending, and by December of that year, the ladies had inked a multi-year contract with Hulu to produce original programming for the international market.
