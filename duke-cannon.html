10% OFF Duke Cannon Discount Codes January 2024

If you want to save and stock up on soap or body wash, you should consider building your own bundle at Duke Cannon. When you bundle four different soaps or body washes, you’ll get 25% off your entire bundle automatically.

To get started, just go to their website. Then, click “Offers” and choose “Build Your Own THICK Bundle” for body wash, or “Build Your Own Soap Bundle” for soap. You can mix and match up to four different soaps or body washes in each bundle.

Once you’re done, just click “Add To Cart.” Bundles can’t be combined with other discount codes. However, if you spend $35, you’ll get free shipping. So if you add another item or two to your cart, you can save on shipping fees, too.
