Calling it QuitsMargaret Cho Files for Divorce From Husband Al Ridenour

It’s over for Margaret Cho and Al Ridenour.

The 46-year-old comedienne filed for divorce from her artist husband after 11 years of marriage on Wednesday, Aug. 12, People has confirmed.

Margaret’s ex-husband Al in 2004.

According to the entertainment news outlet, Margaret and Al, 54, have been separated since September 2014 and cited “irreconcilable differences” as the reason for their split.

When asked about her marriage in a previous interview with Bond Magazine, Margaret said, “It’s very conventional and conservative, I think. I mean we’re such weird people that people just can’t imagine that we would have a conventional marriage. But, yeah, we are very conventional.”

The couple married in 2003 and have no children together.
