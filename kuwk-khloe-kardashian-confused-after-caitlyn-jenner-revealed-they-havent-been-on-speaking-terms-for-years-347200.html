KUWK: Khloe Kardashian Confused After Caitlyn Jenner Revealed They Havent Been On Speaking

Caitlyn Jenner confessed while on I’m a Celebrity….Get Me Out of Here that she and Khloe Kardashian are not really on speaking terms these days and it turns out that the Keeping Up with the Kardashians star is rather confused about that statement. One insider report claims Khloe was ‘blindsided’ by it as that is not really the case.

The transgender icon went as far as to say that she and Khloe haven’t spoken in no less than five or six years!

TMZ reports that Khloe is really confused now since Caitlyn claimed she stopped talking to her after she came out as transgender, something that happened in 2015 so the timeline definitely wouldn’t add up.

And that is not the only thing that doesn’t add up for Khloe since sources tell TMZ that she was actually as supportive as she could be when she learned Caitlyn was trans.

As for the fact that things got tense between them, it might be true but it was only because Cait called out Khloe’s mom, Kris Jenner in her tell-all book The Secrets of My Life when it was released two years ago!

In the memoir, Caitlyn claimed Kris was well aware she was questioning her gender identity while they were married but the momager insisted she had no idea and was shocked to learn that her husband was actually a transgender woman.
