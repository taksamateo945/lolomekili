Is Lucas Leaving When Calls the Heart?

No, Lucas Bouchard is not leaving the show. There is no official announcement about his departure from the show, When Calls the Heart.

Chris McNally who played Lucas Bouchard in the television show When Calls the Heart recently shared a social media post via his Instagram account and wrote:

“I read on the internet that season 10 is premiering July 30, so it must be true! *spoiler it is* Thank you #hearties for your patience!”
