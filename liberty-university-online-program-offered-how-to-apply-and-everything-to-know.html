Liberty University Online; Program Offered, How To Apply

Liberty University online; Program offered, How to apply And Everything to know, Liberty University’s online programs give you the flexibility to study at your own pace, from your home or office, while maintaining a level of structure that will help you stay on the right track toward achieving your educational goals.

You will receive a quality education – that is flexible and affordable – from an accredited institution.

Reasons to (Why) Choose Liberty University?

  • Prepare for a career

Go from just surviving your current job to thriving in your dream career.

  • Enjoy flexible courses

Complete the tasks at your own pace, at a time that suits you every week.

  • Access to academic support

Access to our online library, writing resources, study aids, and IT support.

  • Grow in your faith

Each of our programs is anchored in Bible principles, and our professors see teaching as more than just a job – it’s their mission.

  • financial aid

87% of all Liberty students on an online program are awarded financial aid.

  • Tuition discounts

Available to members of the military service, including active and reserve duty, veterans and their spouses, as well as emergency response personnel.

  • Help with corporate tuition fees

Deferred – If your company pays for your education, you send your grades to the employer who pays you, and then you pay for your classes.
